Jack Rasmussen

Game Designer
My name is Jack Rasmussen, and I'm a passionate game designer with a proven track record of collaborating across platforms and fostering interdisciplinary communication. I specialize in Unity and Unreal Engine, with a focus on multiplayer experiences, system design, and live service mechanics. My goal is to create unforgettable gaming experiences that resonate with players, whether they're playing solo or with friends.

PROJECT WINTER (2019)

I joined the Project Winter team shortly after its 1.0 launch, initially as a Community Manager. Leveraging my familiarity with both the game and its community, I transitioned into a traditional game design role, where I’ve meticulously crafted unique items, roles, character abilities, and core systems. These contributions have strengthened the game’s social deception elements, enhancing overall gameplay balance and player engagement.

  • Developed core features and systems
  • Created diverse, engaging maps
  • Designed monetization and economy models
  • Designed and implemented sandbox items
  • Developed unique roles and character abilities
  • Integrated new social interaction features

Platforms: Steam, Xbox, Playstation, Switch

The Walking Dead: Betrayal (2023)

Building on my experience with Project Winter, I contributed to the foundational design direction for The Walking Dead: Betrayal. My role included creating the essential design documents and pitch materials that helped secure funding for the project. Drawing from social deception elements, I adapted and innovated gameplay mechanics, roles, and character abilities to align with The Walking Dead universe, crafting a fresh take on familiar objectives and interactions within a survival-driven setting. Shortly after securing funding, I transitioned to another project, so my contributions were focused on the initial design and prototyping phase

  • Defined the initial design direction for The Walking Dead: Betrayal
  • Monetization and economy design
  • Created pitch materials and design documentation to help secure project funding
  • Developed early gameplay mechanics, roles, and character abilities tailored to The Walking Dead universe
  • Adapted social deception elements and objectives to fit a survival-focused, post-apocalyptic setting
  • Contributed to design and prototyping phases before transitioning to a new project post-funding

INCUBATION TEAM

The reason I included this project is because I like playing around with both hardware and code in my spare time. I always challenge myself to make these projects extremely accurate, which is part of the fun. Being involved in homebrew development has been a great learning experience so far.
